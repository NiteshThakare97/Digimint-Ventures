What is Minting?

Minting is the process of creating new cryptocurrency coins. It involves verifying transactions on a blockchain network and adding new blocks to the blockchain. Minting is a crucial component of the crypto ecosystem as it creates new coins, which can be traded on various exchanges. What is Mining?

Mining is the process of validating transactions on a blockchain network and adding new blocks to the blockchain. This process requires powerful computers and specialized software to solve complex mathematical problems. Miners are incentivized with newly minted coins, which are added to the total supply of the cryptocurrency.
